Simple Pancake Recipe

This recipe shows you how to make easy and delicious pancakes at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk.
  3. Stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Cook on a hot pan until golden brown.
